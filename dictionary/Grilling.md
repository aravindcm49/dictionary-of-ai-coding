A technique for developing a **design concept** (see [[Design concept]]) with an agent (see [[Agent]]): the agent interviews the user Socratically, one decision at a time, proposing a recommended answer for each. Slows the rush to a finished plan — no **handoff artifact** (see [[Handoff artifact]]) is written until the concept stabilises.

*Usage:* "It went straight to writing the spec and got the cancellation logic wrong." "Grill it first — make it ask you about partial cancels, refunds, and timing before it commits anything to the doc. Cheaper to resolve in conversation than in code."
