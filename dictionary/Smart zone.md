Early in a session (see [[Session]]) the agent (see [[Agent]]) is in a "smart zone" — sharp, focused, recall is good. As the session grows it drifts into a "dumb zone": sloppier, forgetful, more mistakes — and more **faithfulness hallucinations** (see [[Hallucination]]). Same model (see [[Model]]), same harness (see [[Harness]]) — just more context (see [[Context]]). The felt effect of **attention degradation** (see [[Attention degradation]]). Clear (see [[Clearing]]) or compact (see [[Compaction]]) when the session bloats; don't push through.

*Usage:* "It nailed the first three components and just butchered the fourth." "You're out of the smart zone — same model, just deep into the dumb zone now. Compact and reload the plan, the next component will land."
